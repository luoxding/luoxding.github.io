I guess I have a blog now

February 8, 2022 ¬∑ James

When I was setting up my portfolio site I realized that it also has some pretty nifty blogging capabilities too. Well… I guess I have a blog now.

I’m not typically one to channel my energy through writing so I don’t expect to be extremely active on here but it’s nice to have a venue where I can record my thoughts from time to time.

In the meantime I’ll begin to work on putting together some more content to showcase some of my open source work.

Thanks for being here

– jf
